FinGOL Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2019
Effective date: February 11, 2019

Luxembourg Tech School (LTS) asbl ("us", "we" or "our") operates the FinGoL Computer Program in the form of an Educational Game Chatbot (hereinafter referred to as the "Service").

We aim to be completely transparent with how we collect and use personal information we gather about our users. Our users' privacy is important to us and we will protect and respect the personal information of all users who use or access our Service. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data. We use your data to improve the Service and for purely educational purposes. By using or accessing the Service in any manner, you agree to the collection and the use of your information in accordance with this policy.

How does LTS asbl control and process the data?

We are the Data Controller, as we determine the means and/or purposes of the personal information processing. Simultaneously, we are a Data Processor, as we carry out personal information processing tasks, in order to accomplish our purposes. The Data Controller is responsible of controlling that the data of users is processed in a proper manner and shall take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or unauthorized destruction of the data. In addition to the Data Controller, in some cases, the data may be accessible to certain types of persons in charge, involved with the operation of the service (developers, analysts, legal, system administration) or external parties (such as third-party technical service providers, mail carriers, hosting providers, institutions, online platforms) appointed, if necessary, as Data Processors by the owner. The updated list of these parties may be requested from the Data Controller at any time. The data processing is carried out using computers and/or IT enabled tools, following organizational procedures and modes strictly related to the purposes indicated. Currently, the Data Processors are LTS asbl itself and the online platform for developing chatbots Chatfuel (200 Labs, Inc.). Additionally, the online social media and social networking service company, Facebook Inc., is a sub-processor.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

What information does FinGoL collect?

Information You Provide to Us:

We receive and collect any information you consciously provide to us. For example, if you contact us (via our Facebook page, phone, email, letter or form), e.g. for a support request or for responding to a survey: Name, e-mail address, IP address, information on our communication with you and any additional information you provide us with.

Information Collected Automatically:

Chatfuel is a bot platform for creating chatbots for Facebook Messenger. The linkage between Chatfuel and Facebook Messenger enables the storage of system attributes. System attributes are available in every bot and contain all the information the bot receives about Facebook Messenger users. These attributes are provided to us by our Data Processor (Chatfuel), they cannot be modified, and contain the following user's personal information, if available:

Fields Description
Messenger ID User's unique persistent ID. Also known as Facebook's page-scoped user ID
First name User's first name
Last name User's last name
Gender User's gender
Timezone User's timezone
Locale User's Messenger language setting
Ref Value of ref parameter from m.me link user clicked last time
Profile picture User's profile picture URL

The collection of this personal information has no specific purpose; they are stored in our records by default since it is about a platform connected to Facebook Messenger. Our Data Processor, Chatfuel, claims that they do not use this personal data for any purpose other than to provide us, the Data Controller, with this information. Moreover, we, the Data Controller, also allege that we do not use this information for any purpose at all.

We automatically receive and collect information about you whenever you use or access our Service. For example, while using our Service, you will have to input some personal information, such as your language, work experience level and interests. For some entries, ranges of values or hard-coded options are offered, in order to add a degree of anonymity. In the Appendix A, you can find the user's information fields asked in the FinGoL game. Moreover, through our Service you will be asked about other kind of information such as your approximate monthly income and expenses sources. Please note we do not control whether the information resembles reality or not. The main and only purpose of our Service is to teach, guide and advise on how to manage personal finances, therefore, that the data does not completely match the reality does not interfere with the purpose of the game. We do recommend not to use your real personal financial data or any other private data.

Once assured that we have the appropriate security and contractual guarantees to protect your information, we will share this one with:

  • Third parties for legal reasons: we must share your personal information with law enforcement, regulators and other legal parties, in order to comply with the law, enforce our legal claims and/or protect the integrity and security of our Service and our users' information.

Is your personal information secure?

We strive to protect the privacy of your personal information that we have in our records, but unfortunately, we cannot guarantee complete security. Unexpected events such as, unauthorized entry or use, cyber-attacks, hardware or software failure, and other factors, may compromise the security of user information at any time. Since you will access our Service via Facebook Messenger you must prevent unauthorized access to your account and personal information by selecting and protecting your password and/or other sign-on mechanism appropriately and limiting access to your computer or device and browser by signing off after you have finished accessing your account.

How long will we keep your information?

We will store personal information for as long as the user uses our Service. For inactive users during a period of 3 months, their information will be deleted.

What rights and choices do you have?

You have the right, at any time, to know whether your personal data has been stored and can consult the Data Controller to learn about your contents and origin, to verify your accuracy or to ask for them to be supplemented, cancelled, updated or corrected, or for their transformation into anonymous format or to block any data held in violation of the law, as well as to oppose their treatment for any and all legitimate reasons. Requests should be sent to the Data Controller at the contact information set out below. You can also always opt not to disclose information to us by not using or accessing the Service or any contact form towards us. You may stop using the Service at any time. If you remain inactive for a period of 3 months, your information will be immediately and entirely erased from our records. You also have the right to expressly request the deletion of all your information at any time and we will immediately and entirely erase your information from our records. The process to request the deletion of your information may be done by sending us an email to info@techschool.lu expressing that you want us to delete your information from our records.
